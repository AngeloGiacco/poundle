Benefits of using Poundles

Help the council!

Payments made with Poundle can be monitored to see how the local economy is performing. The council can not see who you are and what you are buying but will receive useful information on the number and volume of transactions made on a day. This will greatly help them to measure how efficient certain incentives are and to see when new incentives are required to stimulate the local economy.

Support local businesses!

Poundles can only be spent in local businesses, thus boosting the local economy. Anybody that distrusts large organisations that shuttle their profits into offshore accounts and believes in the importance of the local highstreet should use Poundles to support their local businesses!

Save the planet!

By supporting local businesses, you are not only boosting our town's economy you are also saving the planet. The logistics operations used by companies like Waitrose and Tesco are extremely polluting and damaging to the environment. By spending your poundles in local shops that source their goods locally, you are contributing greatly to reducing carbon emissions and saving our planet!
