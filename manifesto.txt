We believe that all large corporations must be kept in check.
We believe that the key to improving local economies is not in giving these
large companies large tax breaks but rather in supporting local businesses.
We believe that Poundle is a part of the solution in our town of Oundle because
we believe that a local cryptocurrency backed by the British Pound that can only
be spent in local businesses will greatly boost local business.
We think that the system should be trialed in Oundle and then expanded throughout
East Northmaptonshire.
Finally, a platform should be built so that a council or county can easily
create their own local cryptocurrency.
